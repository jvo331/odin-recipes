Spaghetti

Desciption

Spaghetti is a long, thin, solid, cylindrical pasta. It is a staple food of traditional Italian cuisine. Like other pasta, spaghetti is made of milled wheat and water and sometimes enriched with vitamins and minerals.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground meat (beef)
  • 3 tbsps olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 2 tbsps tomato paste
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • pinch crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • pinch of salt and black pepper
  • handful fresh basil leaves
  • 12 oz dried spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Heat oil in large pot over medium-high heat and add meat and cook until browned, about 8 mins
  2. Use a wooden sppon to break up into smaller crumbles
  3. Add onions and cook for about 5 mins, stir every once in a while
  4. Stir in garlic, tomato paste, oregano, and red pepper flakes for about 1 min
  5. Pour in water and scrape off any bits of meat or onion stuck to bottom of pot
  6. Stir in tomatoes, 3/4 tsps of salt, and a generous pinch of black pepper
  7. Bring sauce to a low simmer
  8. Cook, uncovered, at a low simmer for 25 mins
  9. Adjust seasoning to liking
  10. About 15 mins before sauce finishes, bring a large pot of salted water to boil
  11. Cook pasta according to package directions, but check for doneness a min or two before suggested cooking time
  12. Take sauce off heat and stir in basil
  13. Toss in cooked pasta and leave in for 1 min
  14. Toss again and serve with parmesan sprinkled on top
